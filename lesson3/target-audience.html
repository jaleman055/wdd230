Point of the Mountain Chamber

The Point of the Mountain Chamber is a non-profit organization that promotes and supports the local businesses and community in the area. We offer networking opportunities, educational events, advocacy and marketing services for our members.

Target Audience

Our target audience consists of local business owners, entrepreneurs, professionals, and residents who are interested in joining or supporting our chamber of commerce. They are looking for ways to grow their businesses, network with other members, learn new skills, and contribute to the community. They value quality, reliability, innovation, and collaboration.

John Smith

  • Age: 35
  • Gender: Male
  • Occupation: Owner of a local coffee shop
  • Goals: To increase his sales, attract more customers, and expand his business.
  • Frustrations: High competition, lack of marketing skills, limited budget.
  • Motivations: To provide quality service, to be recognized as a leader in his industry, to support other local businesses.

Scenarios:

- John is a coffee shop owner who wants to join the chamber of commerce for various reasons. He wants to get access to exclusive discounts, benefits, and opportunities, learn how to create a successful social media campaign, network with other business owners, sponsor a community event, and get advice or assistance on a business issue or challenge.
